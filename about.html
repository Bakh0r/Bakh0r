Our vision

It all started with a dream, a vision, a purpose: to meet modest women’s desire to wear the clothes that fit the life and times they live in

Our history

Bahorfashion.com launched on Mother’s Day, 2019.We were the first Uzbek fashion platform to deliver online clothes shopping to our target audience in such variety. Born in Tashkent, we now have customers in most countries. We offer contents in 6 languages: Uzbek, Turkish, Arabic, English, French, German. We speak our customers’ language, we recognize their needs, and we know their preferences. Each month, we reach over million visitors from all over the world via our app and desktop platforms. We sell over 100 brands and 20 thousand products, shipping thousands of packages a day to five continents. We employ confident and youthful staff from different nationalities and cultures whose average age is 25.

Our objectives

In order to keep our clothing line innovative and fresh, we need to constantly test out new business ideas and market ideas. Take as much feedback as we can from our customers and other sources and then use that feedback to constantly experiment with new methods and designs and see what works and what doesn’t. Over time, we won’t just be staying on top of trends; we will be initiating them.
